We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Period.

The extension works entirely offline within your browser. Your browsing data never leaves your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect absolutely NO information.

The "Bookmark All Windows" extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data.

2. How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely within your browser:

  • Tab Information: The extension reads tab URLs and titles only when you click the bookmark button. This information is used solely to create bookmarks and is never stored or transmitted anywhere.
  • Bookmarks: Bookmarks are created using your browser's built-in bookmark system. They are stored locally by your browser, not by the extension.
  • No External Communication: The extension does not connect to any external servers, APIs, or services.

3. Browser Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • tabs: Required to read the URLs and titles of your open tabs. This is necessary to create bookmarks for each tab.
  • bookmarks: Required to create bookmark folders and save bookmarks to your browser.

These permissions are used exclusively for the extension's core functionality. No data accessed through these permissions is collected, stored, or shared.

4. Data Storage

The extension does not store any data. All bookmarks created by the extension are stored by your browser's native bookmark system, which you have full control over.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking, or advertising.

6. Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to share. We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

The extension does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Open Source

This extension is open source. You can review the complete source code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
